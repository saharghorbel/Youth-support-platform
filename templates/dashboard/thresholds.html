About Risk Thresholds

Risk thresholds define the criteria used to assess student risk levels in the early warning system.

Threshold Parameters:
  • Attendance Threshold: Minimum acceptable attendance rate (%). Students below this are flagged.
  • Grade Threshold: Minimum acceptable average grade (0-20 scale). Students below this are flagged.
  • Behavior Threshold: Maximum acceptable number of behavior incidents. Students exceeding this are flagged.
  • Missed Appointments Threshold: Maximum acceptable number of missed appointments. Patients exceeding this are flagged.
Important: Only one threshold can be active at a time. Activating a new threshold will automatically deactivate the current one.
Use Cases:
  • Standard Threshold: Default values for normal operation
  • Strict Threshold: Lower thresholds for high-priority monitoring periods
  • Lenient Threshold: Higher thresholds for adjustment periods or special circumstances
  • Custom Threshold: Tailored values for specific schools or programs
