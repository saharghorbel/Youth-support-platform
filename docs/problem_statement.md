# Problem Statement

## Youth Support Platform for Tunisia

### Population
**Target Group**: Children and youth aged 6-25 in Tunisia, with focus on:
- School-aged children (6-18 years)
- Young adults in transition (18-25 years)
- At-risk populations in education and health systems

### Problem
**Core Challenges**:
1. **Educational Disengagement**: Early dropout risk due to poor attendance, declining grades, and behavioral issues
2. **Mental Health Gaps**: Insufficient tracking and follow-up for youth mental health and psychosocial support
3. **Fragmented Data**: Lack of integrated systems to identify at-risk youth early
4. **Limited Resources**: Counselors and educators overwhelmed without decision-support tools

### Decision Makers
**Primary Stakeholders**:
- **School Counselors**: Need prioritized intervention lists and risk assessments
- **Teachers**: Require behavioral and academic trend insights
- **Health Professionals**: Need appointment tracking and adherence monitoring
- **School Administrators**: Require aggregate metrics for resource allocation
- **NGO Coordinators**: Need case management and outcome tracking

### Operational Workflow

#### Scenario 1: Education Early Warning
1. **Intake**: Operator enters student attendance, grades, behavior notes
2. **Assessment**: System calculates risk score based on multiple indicators
3. **Review**: Supervisor reviews flagged students and risk explanations
4. **Planning**: Supervisor creates intervention plan with specific goals
5. **Follow-up**: Track intervention progress and outcomes
6. **Re-evaluation**: Periodic reassessment of risk levels

#### Scenario 2: Health/Mental-Health Follow-Up
1. **Registration**: Operator creates patient record and schedules appointments
2. **Tracking**: System monitors appointment attendance and missed sessions
3. **Detection**: Automatic detection of adherence issues
4. **Alert**: System triggers reminder/referral workflow
5. **Intervention**: Counselor contacts patient and documents follow-up
6. **Outcome**: Track resolution and long-term adherence

### Expected Value

**Measurable Improvements**:
- **Faster Intervention**: Reduce time from risk detection to intervention by 50%
- **Better Follow-up**: Increase follow-up completion rate from 60% to 85%
- **Fewer Dropouts**: Reduce school dropout rate by early identification
- **Resource Efficiency**: Prioritize high-risk cases for limited counselor time
- **Data-Driven Decisions**: Replace ad-hoc assessments with structured indicators
- **Accountability**: Audit trail for all actions and decisions

### Validation Rules

**Success Criteria**:
1. **Data Quality**: >95% of intake records pass validation
2. **Risk Detection**: System identifies known at-risk cases with >80% accuracy
3. **Workflow Completion**: >90% of flagged cases receive intervention plan
4. **Timeliness**: High-risk alerts generated within 24 hours of data entry
5. **User Adoption**: >80% of counselors use system regularly
6. **Audit Compliance**: 100% of actions logged with user, timestamp, and reason

### Constraints and Boundaries

**What This System Does**:
- Provides decision support and risk indicators
- Tracks structured data and workflows
- Generates alerts and recommendations
- Maintains audit trails and compliance

**What This System Does NOT Do**:
- Make autonomous high-impact decisions without human review
- Provide clinical diagnoses or medical advice
- Replace professional judgment of counselors and educators
- Guarantee prediction accuracy (indicators are proxies, not certainties)

### Ethical Considerations

**Privacy**:
- Role-based access to sensitive data
- Anonymization for research and reporting
- Consent assumptions documented
- No data sharing without authorization

**Fairness**:
- Avoid stigmatizing labels in UI
- Explain risk factors transparently
- Allow human override of system recommendations
- Regular bias audits of indicators

**Safety**:
- No automated actions for high-risk cases
- Escalation paths for critical situations
- Clear limitations communicated to users
- Fallback to human decision-making

### Success Metrics

**Operational**:
- Workflow completion rate: >90%
- Data validation pass rate: >95%
- Alert precision: >80%
- System uptime: >99%

**Social Impact Proxies**:
- Intervention plan completion rate
- Time from risk detection to first contact
- Follow-up adherence improvement
- User satisfaction scores
